Our Mission

EWSETA (Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority) is a South African government body that focuses on developing skills and training in the energy and water sectors.Our mission is to help advance the industry by offering a range of training and development opportunities and initiatives.By developing infrastructure,EWSETA aims to build a skilled workforce that can strenghten the energy and water sectors,thereby promoting economic growth and sustainability.Furthermore,the organization believes in empowering the youth by providing opportunities to develop vital skills in these sectors,allowing them to participate fully in South Africa's economic development.Through its various programs and partnerships,EWSETA is making significant progress towards its goals,and significant progress towards its goals,and its work is sure to have a lasting impact on on the industry and the youth in South Africa.
